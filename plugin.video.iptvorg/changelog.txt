Version 1.0.0 (Initial Release) 24th March 2025
Initial Release of IPTV-Org Addon.

Features Live TV

PIN Protection: Protects adult content behind a PIN.

Developer Mode: Enables a developer mode that allows bypassing the PIN and extra debugging.

Favourites Management: Users can add or remove channels to/from their favourites.

M3U Channel Integration: Fetches IPTV channels via M3U.

Settings Menu: Allows users to update TV channels, set/reset PIN, toggle Developer Mode, and view changelog.
